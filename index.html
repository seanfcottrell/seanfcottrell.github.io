About Me

Recent graduate from the Michigan State University Departments of Mathematics and Economics. Current PhD student in the Department of Computational Mathematics, Sciences, and Engineering. Also currently a research assistant in the MSU Department of Mathematics WeiLab. Feel free to check out my Linkedin!

Research

My research is primarily concerned with the application of advanced mathematical methods to the analysis of biomolecular / biomedical data. Specifically, the topological analysis of transcriptome data, including single cell, spatial, and multi-modal data; as well as the design and implementation of Machine Learning and Deep Learning techniques for computational biology. More broadly, I am also interested in mathematical biophysics and computational drug design. Below is a list of recent papers, with a more detailed accounting of my work available on my Google Scholar page. The links in the header also provide some brief descriptions of recent projects.
