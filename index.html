Research

My research is primarily concerned with the application of advanced mathematical methods to the analysis of biomolecular / biomedical data. Specifically, the topological analysis of transcriptome data, including single cell, spatial, and multi-modal data; as well as the design and implementation of Machine Learning and Deep Learning techniques for computational biology. More broadly, I am also interested in mathematical biophysics and computational drug design. Below is a list of recent papers, with a more detailed accounting of my work available on my Google Scholar page.
